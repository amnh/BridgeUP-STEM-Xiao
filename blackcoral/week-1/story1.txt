At the bottom of Milford Sound in New Zealand lived a black coral named...
Philip Applebottom. Philip was a shark enthusiast. He loved to stare up at the sharks with their majestic fins. He wished that he had fins like a shark. Every night he dreamed of swimming with the sharks, flapping his amazing fins and singing fun shark songs with his lovely shark pals.

One day a fairy came and decided to grant him one wish! And guess what he picked? He became a shark! He swam and flapped his amazing fins and sang fun shark songs all day long.  But when it came time to eat, he did not want to eat fish!
